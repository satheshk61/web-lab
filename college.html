About Us

Adhiyamaan College of Engineering (ACE) is a premier educational institution committed to academic excellence and innovation. Established in 1987, we have been at the forefront of technical education in Tamil Nadu for over three decades.

Courses Offered

  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Information Technology (IT)

  • Internet of Things (IoT)
